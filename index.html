Minh Nhat Vu

Minh is Co-founded & and Chief Science Officer at VinRobotics. He is also a Visiting Assistant Professor at VinUniversity (CAIR - Center for AI Research), and a Scientist at TU Wien (ACIN). His work spans real-time optimal control (MPC / trajectory optimization / motion planning) and robot learning (VLA/VLM for manipulation, grasping, and navigation), with an emphasis on deployable systems. He focuses on the integration that makes it work end-to-end, most recently improving the humanoid locomotion with fast solving closed-chain kinematics algorithm, low-latency middleware, realtime EtherCAT control, and RL refinement.
